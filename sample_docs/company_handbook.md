# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Sample)

This is a sample document used to demo and evaluate the RAG assistant.

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees are entitled to **20 days of paid annual leave** per calendar year.
Annual leave must be requested at least **7 days in advance** through the HR portal.
Unused leave can be carried over, but a maximum of **5 days** may be carried into the next year.

Sick leave is separate and capped at **10 days** per year. A medical certificate is
required for sick leave longer than **3 consecutive days**.

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are **9:30 AM to 6:30 PM**, Monday to Friday.
Employees may work remotely up to **2 days per week** with manager approval.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses are reimbursed within **15 business days** of submitting a claim.
Claims must include a valid receipt and be submitted within **30 days** of the expense.
Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to **₹1,500 per day**.

## Probation and Notice Period

New employees serve a probation period of **6 months**.
After confirmation, the notice period for resignation is **2 months**.
During probation, the notice period is **15 days**.

## IT and Security

Passwords must be changed every **90 days** and must be at least **12 characters** long.
Report any security incident to security@acme.example within **24 hours**.
Company laptops must have disk encryption enabled.
